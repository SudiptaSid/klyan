Firm Money

A well managed accounting system is essential to the growth of your firm. Klyant provides your team with all the tools they need to provide the firm with the KPIs and Management Accounts that will inform strategy.

  • Beautiful Invoicing
  • Bank Account Reconciliation
  • Receipt Allocation
  • Purchase Ledger Management
  • Transfer Between Office Accounts
  • Cheque Printing
  • Batch Transfer Files

Client Money

Generic accounting systems cannot properly manage client money because they were not designed for law firms. Klyant seamlessly manages client money separately to firm money in one incredible application. It tracks the movement of client money into and out of the client account between the client and office accounts. It just makes sense.

  • Record all Client Account Transactions
  • Transfer between client accounts
  • Transfer between the client and office account
  • Transfer between matters

Outlay

The management of outlay and disbursements can be a real nightmare for law firms. Not with Klyant.

  • Record unbilled outlay
  • Auto populate invoices with outlay
  • Link outlay to purchase ledger
  • Record outay receipts
  • Report on outlay expensiture with ease

Financial Reporting

We offer a comprehensive suite of management and financial reporting. Our report suite even includes a regulation section to help your firm prepare for audits. All reports can be exported to excel and pdf.
